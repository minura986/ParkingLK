Parking Web Application - Feature Breakdown

=========================================
1. USER (DRIVER / CUSTOMER)
=========================================
Users are the primary consumers of the platform who need to find, book, and pay for parking slots.

Features:
- Authentication & Profile: Register, log in, and manage personal profile information.
- Search & View Car Parks: Browse the system for available car parks, view their details, check real-time slot availability, and see EV charging capabilities.
- Book Parking Slots: Make advance or on-the-spot reservations for parking slots.
- Flexible Check-in (Early Check-in): Capable of checking in early. The system automatically handles early check-ins without penalty up to a certain time, and calculates extra charges dynamically if they arrive significantly early (e.g., Rs. 10 per 10-minute block).
- Payments: Process payments securely for the parking bookings.
- My Bookings: Dashboard to view active bookings, upcoming reservations, and past historical bookings.
- Notifications: Receive real-time alerts (via a notification bell/dropdown in the navbar) regarding booking statuses and payments.

=========================================
2. CAR PARK OWNER
=========================================
Owners manage physical car parks and monetize their parking spaces through the platform.

Features:
- Owner Dashboard: A central hub displaying total earnings, active parking slots, and overall car park performance.
- Manage Car Parks: They can add new car park locations to the platform (which requires Super Admin approval before going live), as well as update existing car park details (pricing, EV charging fees, location types).
- Manage Slots: Allocate and configure individual parking slots within each of their approved carparks.
- Manage Attendants: Register and assign parking attendants to their car parks. Attendants have their own portal to manage ground operations like scanning booking QR codes to activate them.
- Manage/View Bookings: Real-time visibility into who has booked slots, current site occupancy, and transaction details for their specific car parks.
- Revenue & Payout Tracking: Access an "Owner Revenue" section to track their earnings, view generated revenue, and check the payout history processed by the Super Admin.

=========================================
3. SUPER ADMIN
=========================================
Super Admins are the platform managers who oversee all operations, users, and financial settlements.

Features:
- Super Admin Dashboard: High-level overview of the entire system's metrics (total users, total revenue, platform health).
- Comprehensive User Management: Navigate through a dedicated dropdown menu to easily filter, view, and manage all platform roles (Users, Car Owners, Attendants, Super Admins). They can block/unblock users or change roles.
- Car Park Approval Flow: Act as the gatekeeper for new car parks. When an owner adds a new car park, it goes to "Pending" status. The Super Admin reviews and approves it to make it active and visible to Users.
- Global Payout System: Manage financial settlements. Super Admins track the global revenue generated by bookings and process payouts directly to the Car Park Owners. They also have an interface to track which owners have been paid and what is pending.
- Global Monitoring: View all car parks, bookings, and system activities across the entire platform to ensure smooth operations and resolve disputes.
